Frequently Asked Questions

What happens in the first session?

+

The first session is approximately 30 minutes. It covers orientation, your baseline AI competency assessment, setting expectations, and your first guided exercises are assigned.

How much time do I need each week?

+

About 20 minutes for the weekly check-in, plus 1 to 2 hours of self-paced exercises and tutorials. The pace is designed for working professionals.

Do I need technical experience?

+

No. The programme is calibrated to your starting level. Complete beginners are welcome. If you are more experienced, the exercises and material will adjust to challenge you appropriately.

What if I need to miss a week?

+

The programme pauses and resumes when you are ready. No penalty, no pressure. Life happens, and the programme is designed around that reality.

What platform do you use?

+

Google Meet or Microsoft Teams, your choice. Whichever is most convenient for you.

What do I get at the end?

+

A measurable before-and-after competency assessment showing your growth across six dimensions. Plus the AI Badge digital credential that you can share on LinkedIn and with your employer.

Can my employer sponsor this?

+

Yes. Contact Victor directly for corporate arrangements, team packages, or invoice-based payment.
