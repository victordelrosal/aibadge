Programme Guidelines

Last updated: 12 May 2026

🎯
Self-paced, end-to-end. AI Badge is currently a self-paced programme. You progress through tutorials, exercises, and the capability assessment on your own time, at your own pace. No fixed sessions, no calendar.
🔑
Access is by invitation. The programme is currently invitation-only. National College of Ireland accounts (@ncirl.ie) get instant access on sign-in. Others can request access by emailing victor@fiveinnolabs.com.
📚
How it works. Complete the baseline assessment, work through the tutorials in your dashboard, submit your exercise artefacts. Your progress is tracked automatically. When you've completed the programme, take the final assessment to see your before-and-after.
💬
Support. Questions, feedback, or stuck on an exercise? Email victor@fiveinnolabs.com. There are no scheduled check-ins right now: support is asynchronous.
🔒
Your data. Your account, submissions, and progress are stored in Firebase. See our Privacy Policy for how we handle your data.
🛡️
Consumer protection. EU consumer rights apply. See our Terms & Conditions for full details.
